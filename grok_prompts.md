# Grok Research Prompts

Here are optimized prompts you can use with Grok to research the best tools and settings for your project.

## 1. Polish ASMR Voice Settings (ElevenLabs)

**Prompt:**
> "Search X (formerly Twitter) and Reddit for the absolute best ElevenLabs voice settings for Polish ASMR. Specifically looking for:
> 1. Recommended Stability and Similarity Boost values for whispering/soft spoken Polish.
> 2. Specific Voice IDs or characteristics (e.g. 'soft female', 'warm tone') that work well with the Polish language model.
> 3. Any tips for 'breaking' the prosody to make it sound more human and less robotic in Polish."

## 2. Best AI Models for Roleplay Scenarios

> Compare 'Claude 4.5 Opus', 'DeepSeek V3', and 'GPT-5' (if available) specifically for:
